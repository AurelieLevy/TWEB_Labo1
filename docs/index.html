Commits' visual

Here's a chart with some informations about the authors of an Github's repository and theirs commits.
You will find at first the name of the authors. The size of their box is the percentage of commits made by them. By clicking on it, the commits associated with the author you had choose will be displayed. If you leave your mouse on it, you will have some informations about it.
Use the left click to explore the chart and the right click to come back.
